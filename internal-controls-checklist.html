Inside Your Internal Controls Checklist

Our comprehensive Internal Controls Checklist is based on the COSO framework and industry best practices. It covers all five components of an effective internal control system to help you prevent fraud, ensure compliance, and protect your organization's assets.

Control Environment

  • Organizational structure and governance
  • Management philosophy and operating style
  • Integrity and ethical values
  • Commitment to competence

Risk Assessment

  • Identify and analyze risks
  • Fraud risk assessment
  • Risk tolerance determination
  • Change management procedures

Control Activities

  • Authorization and approval policies
  • Segregation of duties
  • Physical controls and safeguards
  • Reconciliations and reviews

Information & Communication

  • Financial reporting processes
  • Documentation standards
  • Internal communication channels
  • External reporting requirements

Monitoring Activities

  • Ongoing monitoring procedures
  • Separate evaluations and audits
  • Reporting deficiencies
  • Continuous improvement processes

Technology Controls

  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data security and backup
  • System change management
  • IT general controls
